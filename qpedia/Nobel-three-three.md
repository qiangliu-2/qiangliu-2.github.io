# Universities with 3+ Science Nobelist Affiliations & 3+ Doctoral Graduates

Among 53 universities with three-plus [science Nobelist affiliations](ql_md_template.html?my.md=qpedia/Nobel-affiliations.md) and 53 institutions with three-plus science [Nobelist doctoral graduates](ql_md_template.html?my.md=qpedia/Nobel-graduates.md), 39 universities are in both groups.

Harvard tops the list with 74 count, while Cambridge comes as second with 60. The US dominates the top 10 with eight entries, while the United Kingdom contributes two.

Ivy League occupies six spots in the top 39 (four in the top 14), a solid showing undoubtedly. **Cornell**, a school that tends to be underappreciated, ranks only behind Harvard, Columbia, and Princeton.

Two universities, Kyoto University and Nagoya University, make into the list.
